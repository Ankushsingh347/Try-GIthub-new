About Suraksha 360°

Suraksha 360° is an AI-powered real-time disaster information and crisis management platform. It offers predictive alerts, live updates, interactive maps, safe zone navigation, and emergency communication tools. Built to minimize panic and maximize preparedness, Suraksha 360° helps citizens, authorities, and communities respond faster and smarter during natural disasters.

Key Features:

  • 📡 Real-time multi-disaster monitoring (earthquake, flood, landslide, cyclone)
  • 🔔 AI-powered early warning notifications
  • 🗺️ Interactive map with live disaster data and safe zones
  • 📞 Emergency contact access and quick SOS features
  • 🌐 Crowdsourced incident reporting
